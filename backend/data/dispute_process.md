# Card Transaction Dispute Process - Dummy Bank

If a customer notices an unauthorized or incorrect card transaction:

1. Immediately block or temporarily freeze the card from mobile app or call support.
2. Raise a dispute within 30 days of transaction date for card-present disputes.
3. For card-not-present (online) unauthorized transactions, raise dispute as soon as possible, preferably within 3 business days.

Required details for dispute:
- Last 4 digits of card
- Transaction date
- Transaction amount
- Merchant name (if visible)
- Brief description of issue

Do not ask customers for OTP, PIN, CVV, or full card number.

Resolution timeline:
- Initial acknowledgement: within 24 hours
- Provisional credit (where applicable): up to 10 business days
- Final resolution: generally within 45 to 90 days based on network/merchant response
