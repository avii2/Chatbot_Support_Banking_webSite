Card Fees and Charges - Dummy Bank

Credit Card Joining Fee:
- Platinum Card: INR 999 + taxes
- Signature Card: INR 2,999 + taxes

Annual Fee:
- Platinum Card: INR 999 + taxes
- Signature Card: INR 2,999 + taxes

Annual fee waiver criteria:
- Platinum Card: waived if annual spend >= INR 1,50,000
- Signature Card: waived if annual spend >= INR 4,00,000

Cash advance fee:
- 2.5% of cash amount or INR 500, whichever is higher

Late payment fee (credit cards):
- Statement balance up to INR 500: Nil
- INR 501 to INR 5,000: INR 500
- INR 5,001 to INR 10,000: INR 750
- Above INR 10,000: INR 1,200

Card replacement fee:
- INR 250 + taxes for domestic dispatch
