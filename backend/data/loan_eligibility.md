# Loan Eligibility - Dummy Bank

## Personal Loan

Eligibility summary:
- Age: 21 to 60 years at loan maturity
- Net monthly income: at least INR 25,000 for salaried applicants
- Employment stability: minimum 12 months total work experience and 6 months in current organization
- Credit score guideline: 700 or above generally preferred

Typical personal loan range: INR 50,000 to INR 20,00,000.
Typical tenure: 12 to 60 months.

## Home Loan

Eligibility summary:
- Age: 21 to 65 years at loan maturity
- Combined applicant income allowed for co-applicants
- Credit score guideline: 725 or above preferred for best pricing
- FOIR (fixed obligations to income ratio) typically should remain below 50%

Home loan processing fee: up to 0.35% of loan amount + taxes, subject to minimum INR 5,000.

Prepayment:
- Floating-rate home loans for individual borrowers: no prepayment penalty.
- Fixed-rate loans: prepayment charges may apply as per sanction letter.
