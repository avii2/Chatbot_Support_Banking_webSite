KYC Requirements - Dummy Bank

Accepted Proof of Identity (any one):
1) Passport
2) National ID card
3) Driver's license

Accepted Proof of Address (any one):
1) Utility bill not older than 3 months
2) Passport
3) Rental agreement registered with local authority

PAN or tax ID is mandatory for interest-bearing accounts.

For minor accounts, the guardian's KYC is required.

KYC refresh cycle:
- High-risk customers: every 2 years
- Medium-risk customers: every 8 years
- Low-risk customers: every 10 years

Customers should never share OTP, PIN, internet banking password, or full card details during KYC calls.
