# Accounts FAQ - Dummy Bank

## Savings Account Minimum Balance

- Urban branches: minimum average monthly balance is INR 5,000.
- Semi-urban branches: minimum average monthly balance is INR 2,500.
- Rural branches: minimum average monthly balance is INR 1,000.

If the minimum average monthly balance is not maintained, a non-maintenance fee of INR 350 + taxes may apply for that month.

## Account Opening

To open a savings account, customers must complete eKYC or full KYC, provide a valid mobile number, and complete video verification when requested.

## Account Statement

- e-Statements are free.
- Physical statements beyond one free copy per quarter are charged INR 75 per statement.

## Dormant Account Reactivation

An account is marked dormant after 24 months of no customer-initiated transaction. Reactivation requires identity verification and a signed reactivation request at branch or through secure video verification.
